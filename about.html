Your friendly assistant for tracking attendance easily.

Attendance Buddy helps students and professionals manage and track attendance efficiently. Get insights, projections, and simulations to stay on top of your attendance goals without any stress.

Key Features:

  • Easy access and gives accurate results
  • Predicts future attendance based on your inputs
  • Tells you how many classes you need to reach your target percentage
  • Safe zone alerts to avoid falling below required attendance
  • Mobile-friendly and simple interface
